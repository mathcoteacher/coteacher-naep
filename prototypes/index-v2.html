Design Aesthetic Options

Same Flicker animation, 5 different visual identities

Ratio and state will be dynamic from geolocation. Text matches the approved format.

A. Newsprint


Black ink on warm off-white. One red accent for the numbers. NYT data journalism feel — authoritative, no-nonsense.

B. Bruise


Warm amber figures turn cold bruised purple. Dark background. Visceral and uncomfortable — matches the gravity.

C. Chalk


Chalky white figures on dark green-black board. Muted sage accent. Literally a classroom — where this problem lives.

D. Ember


Hot orange-red on near-white. Figures glow like embers then go to cold charcoal. Urgency without being garish.

E. Blueprint


Cream/tan figures on deep navy. Like architectural blueprints. Serious, technical, institutional — but warm, not cold.
